PRO TIPS FOR RECORDING
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TECHNICAL:
- Record at 1.5x or 2x speed, edit to 4-5 min
- Use Loom or similar for easy recording
- Add timestamps in video editor for quick cuts
- Clean audio = wireless mic or good internal mic
- 1080p minimum, 4K if possible

PERFORMANCE:
- Practice the script 3x before recording
- Smile at start - first 3 seconds matter on LinkedIn
- Speak clearly, slightly slower than normal
- Pause after key moments for impact
- End with confident energy

EDITING:
- Add subtle background music (keep it low)
- Cut loading times, keep action moments
- Add text overlays for key features
- Use transitions between sections
- End with clear CTA

BEST TIMES TO POST:
- LinkedIn: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 12-2pm
- Twitter/X: Anytime, engage within first hour
- Instagram: Evenings, 7-9pm
