SKRIPTA - Demo Script (4-5 min)
===================================
START: From scratch - landing page
END: Shareable research dashboard

Flow:
Landing → Sign up → Create project → Add interview → 
Extract insights → Go to affinity map → 
AI group → Generate persona → Share


HOOK (0:00-0:15)
------------------
[Screen: Landing page]

"User research doesn't have to be painful. Let me show you."


PART 1: SIGN UP & PROJECT (0:15-0:45)
----------------------------------------
[Screen: Sign up flow]

"Create an account. Takes 10 seconds."

[Create new project: "Mobile App Research Q1"]

"Create your project. Add your team. Roles - owner, editor, viewer."

[Open team dialog - invite member]

"Collaborate in real-time. See who's online. Changes sync instantly."


PART 2: ADD INTERVIEW (0:45-1:30)
------------------------------------
[Screen: Add interview]

"Drop your interview audio here. Or paste a transcript."

[Upload/paste]

"Our AI transcribes. Identifies speakers. Timestamps everything."

[Show transcription with speakers highlighted]

"One click to analyze. AI extracts insights automatically."


PART 3: EXTRACT INSIGHTS (1:30-2:00)
----------------------------------------
[Screen: Interview detail with insights]

"AI found pain points, quotes, insights. All classified."

[Highlight different types with colors]

"Or highlight text yourself. Classify it. Your call."


PART 4: AFFINITY MAP (2:00-3:00)
------------------------------------
[Screen: Click "Open Affinity Map"]

"Every insight becomes a sticky. Drag to organize."

[Show stickies on canvas]

"But here's where it gets interesting."

[Open AI Features panel]

"AI analyzes patterns. Suggests clusters. With confidence scores."

[Select suggestion, create cluster]

"One click. AI created the cluster. Named it. Based on content."


PART 5: PERSONA (3:00-3:30)
-------------------------------
[Screen: Click Generate Persona]

"AI creates a user persona. From all your data."

[Wait for generation]

"Goals. Pain points. Behaviors. Quote. Everything synthesized."


PART 6: SHARE (3:30-4:30)
----------------------------
[Screen: Click Share]

"Share with stakeholders. No sign-up required for them."

[Show shareable link]

"Presentation mode. Keyboard navigation."

[Navigate slides]

"Dashboard view. Stats, insights, personas."

[Show overview mode]

"Password protection. Expiration. Full control."


CLOSING (4:30-5:00)
---------------------
"From interview to insights to shareable dashboard. In minutes."

[End card: Try at [your-url]]


---

TIPS:
- Use Cmd/Ctrl + clicks to speed up
- Use keyboard shortcuts on canvas (V=vote, C=cluster, S=select)
- Record at 1.5x speed, edit down to 4 min
- Add subtle background music
- Show timestamps on video timeline for editing
